Top Picks

Santorini Highlights

Five must-see spots around the island to pair sunsets with strolls, beach time, and village views.

Oia Village

Blue-domed churches, cliffside lanes, and the island’s most iconic sunset vantage.

Fira Caldera

Café terraces overlooking the caldera, lively shops, and a cable car down to the old port.

Red Beach

Dramatic crimson cliffs, clear Aegean water, and snorkeling-friendly coves.

Pyrgos

Hilltop village with quiet alleys, panoramic views, and a relaxed taverna scene.

Akrotiri

Minoan archaeological site paired with views of the caldera and the nearby lighthouse.

Kyoto Highlights

Historic shrines, mossy gardens, and lantern-lit streets to wander slowly.

Fushimi Inari

Thousand torii gates climbing the hillside, best explored at dawn or dusk.

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

Towering bamboo paths, riverside cafés, and nearby temples wrapped in greenery.

Kinkaku-ji

The Golden Pavilion reflecting over its pond, surrounded by meticulously kept gardens.

Gion

Lantern-lit lanes, teahouses, and glimpses of traditional geiko culture at twilight.

Philosopher’s Path

Stone walkway along a canal lined with cherry trees, perfect for slow seasonal strolls.

Patagonia Highlights

Glaciers, granite towers, and wide-open skies tailor-made for hikers and photographers.

Torres del Paine

Iconic granite spires above vivid lagoons, best seen at sunrise on classic treks.

Grey Glacier

Immense ice fields calving into steel-blue waters, reached by boat or viewpoint hikes.

French Valley

Suspended bridges and soaring cliffs with condors circling overhead on the W trek.

Lago Nordenskjöld

Turquoise lake fringed by wildflowers and windswept viewpoints of the Paine Massif.

Puerto Natales

Gateway town with fjord sunsets, hearty cuisine, and cozy lodges before the trails.

Marrakech Highlights

Markets, gardens, and palaces that mix Moorish craft with desert light and color.

Jemaa el-Fnaa

Evening food stalls, storytellers, and rooftop cafés overlooking the buzzing square.

Jardin Majorelle

Cobalt blue art deco villa, cactus gardens, and the nearby Yves Saint Laurent museum.

Bahia Palace

Intricate zellij tilework, cedar ceilings, and quiet courtyards reflecting royal opulence.

Medersa Ben Youssef

Historic Quranic college famed for its carved stucco, cedar lattice, and serene pool.

Atlas Mountains

Day trips to Imlil or Ourika Valley for cool air, Berber villages, and valley hikes.
